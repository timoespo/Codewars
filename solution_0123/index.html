Each fighter takes turns attacking the other and whoever kills the other first is victorious. Death is defined as having health <;= 0. Each fighter will be a Fighter object/instance. See the Fighter class below in your chosen language. Both health and damagePerAttack (damage_per_attack for python) will be integers larger than 0. You can mutate the Fighter objects. Your function also receives a third argument, a string, with the name of the fighter that attacks first.
